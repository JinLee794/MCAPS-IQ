# Milestone Governance

## When to Use

Weekly cadence during active delivery (Stage 4). CSAM reviews milestone health, prepares customer-safe status, and flags risks.

## Steps

1. **Pull milestone status** — Check CRM for committed milestones. Note any date drift.
2. **Review meeting notes** — Scan recent `knowledge/meetings/` entries for the customer. Any blockers or escalations?
3. **Check action items** — Are outstanding actions progressing? Flag overdue items.
4. **Prepare status bullets** — Customer-safe summary of progress, risks, and next steps.
5. **Internal risk queue** — Separately document internal risks not shared with customer.
6. **Update weekly digest** — Add summary to `knowledge/weekly/` for team visibility.

## Expected Outcome

- Customer-safe status ready for governance meeting
- Internal risk queue with owner and remediation path
- Weekly digest updated for team

## Common Pitfalls

- Status based only on CRM (missing meeting context and verbal updates)
- Risk surfaced too late (should flag at first sign, not after escalation)
- No written record (governance happens verbally but nothing captured)
